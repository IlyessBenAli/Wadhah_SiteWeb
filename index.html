What Can I Do Best ?

Online Courses

Welcome to our online Arabic language courses! We offer comprehensive lessons and resources to help you learn and master the Arabic language.

Parent Communication

Parent communication is a vital aspect of my teaching service. It involves establishing and maintaining open lines of communication with the parents or guardians of my students.

Lesson Plans

Lesson plans are structured outlines that guide your learning journey. They provide a clear roadmap for each session, outlining the topics to be covered, learning objectives, activities, and assessments.

Translation

I offer professional document translation services from French to Arabic as part of my teaching portfolio. Whether it's translating educational materials, communication with non-French-speaking parents, or any other documents, I ensure accurate and culturally sensitive translations.
